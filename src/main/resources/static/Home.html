The IBolit clinic selects its specialists, with a lot of care, so that you can feel safe and trust us! Concerning emergency cases, the indicated facilities are equipped to deal with the necessary demands for each and every case. We offer a fully organized section that can deal with minor surgeries and other injuries, such as orthopedic cases. We are, also, well-equipped to treat every pathological case, being able to provide oxygen support, urgent IV treatment, blood and urine tests, vaccinations, ear cleaning, sea urchin extractions, etc. We cover all age groups and are experienced in pediatric cases, the majority of which we assume from beginning to end, fully and efficiently. All the above are managed from our medical and paramedical staff, in the basis of primary care medicine. In case it is considered necessary, the patient will be further provided with specialized doctors, who are external partners of the clinic, or with admission to a private or public clinics, if it is so suggested by the treating doctor.
